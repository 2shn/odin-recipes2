Pho ga recipe

Description

Chicken Pho (Phở Gà) is a fragrant Vietnamese noodle soup featuring a light, clear broth made by simmering chicken (often thighs or whole chicken) with charred ginger, onions, and toasted spices like star anise and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices (cloves, fennel, star anise, coriander and cinnamon)
  • Onion and ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Pho

Steps

  1. Char onion and ginger
  2. Simmer everything gently for 1.5 hrs
  3. Strain, shred chicken, serve with Toppings!
