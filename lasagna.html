Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring layers of wide, flat noodles (lasagne) alternated with fillings like ragù (meat sauce), béchamel, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Pasta sauce
  • Beef & sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Tomato parse
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
